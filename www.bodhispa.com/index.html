Tread Lightly

At BODHI we take a ‘tread lightly’ approach by using all-natural cruelty-free skin care products in our treatments, recycling where possible, and minimising water use and general waste. We also employ only natural cleaning products to clean each of our wellness locations, across Perth and the South West.

All-Natural Australian Beauty

We are proud to use only the finest Australian clean beauty products in our wellbeing and beauty therapies; all-natural, botanical, vegan and cruelty free.
